3 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Simple, tasty 3 ingredient peanut butter cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Egg

Method

  1. Mix ingredients together
  2. Roll into balls and put on baking tray. Flatten out with a fork to cook evenly.
  3. Bake in 180c oven for 10 minutes or until edges start to turn brown
