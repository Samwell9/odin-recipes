Chicken Wraps

Description

Easy chicken wraps good for meal preps

Ingredients

  • 400g Chicken Thighs
  • 4 Wraps
  • 1 Head of Iceburg lettuce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey

Method

  1. Marinate chicken and put in fridge for 15 minutes or while you wash and prepare lettuce leaves
  2. Cook chicken in fry pan
  3. Set up wraps and assemble
